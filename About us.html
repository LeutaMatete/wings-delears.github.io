About us

Company background

Founded in 2010, Wings Arms Delear has established itself as a leading provider of high-quality weapons and defense solutions. Our journey began with a vision to supply reliable and advanced weaponry to security forces and private entities worldwide.
Our Founders: Reabetsoe Lekhooa and Seeiso Chele, both veterans with extensive experience in the defense sector, recognized the need for a trustworthy and innovative weapons supplier. Their combined expertise and commitment to excellence laid the foundation for our company.
Milestones:
2012: Secured our first major contract with a national defense agency, marking our entry into the global market.
2015: Expanded our product line to include cutting-edge technology and advanced weaponry systems.
2018: Opened our state-of-the-art manufacturing facility, enhancing our production capabilities and ensuring the highest quality standards.
2021: Achieved ISO 9001 certification, reflecting our dedication to quality management and customer satisfaction. Our Mission: To provide superior weapons and defense solutions that ensure the safety and security of our clients. We are committed to innovation, quality, and integrity in all our operations.
